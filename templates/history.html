Brief History of the Potato

  • Condensed and paraphrased from Wikipedia: URL

    • Potatoes were originally domesticated as early as 2500 B.C. Potatoes were used by the Inca Empire (South America).

    The Columbian exchange past 1588, carried multiple goods from the American continent. One of this goods was the potato.

    The potato was a staple crop of Ireland many years after the Columbian Exchange. In 1845, potatoes were infested with a disease that ruined most of the crop. This event lasted 7 years.

    Nearly one million people left Ireland towards the United States, Great Britain, Canada, and elsewhere. Many irish communities can trace their origin to this diaspora.
